FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is CND Poland 2026?
CND Poland 2026 takes place on October 22, 2026 at Varso Tower, Chmielna 73, 00-801 Warsaw. See the Venue section for the full address, map, and how to get there.
How do I get to the venue?
Varso Tower is in the heart of Warsaw and easy to reach. Warszawa Centralna railway station is a 2-minute walk away, and the Rondo ONZ metro station (M2 line) is right next to the venue. Warsaw Chopin Airport (WAW) is about 10 km from the city centre, and paid parking is available in the Varso Tower garage. See the Venue section for a map and directions.
How much does a ticket cost?
Tickets are on sale now, with early-bird pricing available for a limited time. Current prices are listed on our ticket page — grab the early-bird rate before it's gone.
Is there a virtual attendance option?
We are focused on an in-person experience. Sessions may be recorded and published after the event.
Can I submit a talk?
The Call for Papers closed on June 1, 2026, so submissions are no longer being accepted. Thank you to everyone who proposed a talk! Our program committee is now reviewing submissions, and selected speakers will be notified by email. See the CFP section for what happens next.
How can I become a sponsor?
We'd love to hear from you. Download our 2026 sponsorship prospectus (PDF), head to the Contact section, or email us at organizers@cloudnativedayspoland.org.
Is there a Code of Conduct?
Yes. Please read our Code of Conduct. We are committed to providing a safe and welcoming environment for everyone.
Where can I find the legal documents?
You can review our Privacy Policy (PDF) (English), our Conference Terms of Service (PDF) (English) and the Regulations for the Provision of Electronic Services (PDF) (English).
Will there be food and drinks?
Yes, lunch and refreshments will be provided for all attendees, plus great coffee throughout the day and an after-party to keep the community spirit going.
