Code of Conduct

CND Poland is dedicated to providing a harassment-free conference experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion, or technology choices.

Expected Behaviour

  • Be respectful and considerate in your speech and actions
  • Attempt collaboration before conflict
  • Refrain from demeaning, discriminatory, or harassing behaviour and speech
  • Be mindful of your surroundings and fellow participants

Unacceptable Behaviour

  • Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination in any form
  • Verbal abuse, offensive comments, or inappropriate jokes
  • Sustained disruption of talks or other events
  • Unwelcome physical contact or attention
  • Publishing others' private information without consent

Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behaviour, please report it to any member of the organizing team or email us via our contact page. All reports will be handled confidentially.

Enforcement

Participants asked to stop any harassing behaviour are expected to comply immediately. Organizers may take any action deemed necessary, including warning the offender or expulsion from the conference.

This Code of Conduct is based on the Berlin Code of Conduct.
