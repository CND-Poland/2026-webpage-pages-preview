Team

Meet the team behind Cloud Native Day Poland 2026. We are a passionate group of cloud native practitioners, community leaders, and conference veterans with over a decade of cumulative experience organizing in-person conferences and meetups across Poland and beyond.

Our team brings together CNCF Ambassadors, Docker Captains, Java Champions, and seasoned architects who live and breathe cloud native technologies every day. United by a shared mission to grow the cloud native ecosystem in Poland, we work to create an event that delivers real technical depth, fosters meaningful connections, and welcomes everyone — from first-time attendees to industry experts.
